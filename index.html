Common Economic Protocols

The Common Economic protocols are rules for voluntary interactions based on individual ownership, property rights, functioning free markets and free contracts. You can use them for voluntary interactions as an alternative law system. These Common Economic Protocols were placed under public domain by Andre Goldman (2005) - contains: Common Economic Protocols & The Maxims
